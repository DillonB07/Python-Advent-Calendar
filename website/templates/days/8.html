Semaphore - a system of sending messages by holding the arms or two flags or poles in certain positions according to an alphabetic code - Wikipedia The morse code translator you made on Day 2 has been cracked. You need to make a new one! Take the user's input and make an animation of it in semaphore. Use the above Wikipedia entry to see what different characters are in flag semaphoric language.
