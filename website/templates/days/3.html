Welcome to the third door! It's now Friday 3rd December, and so it is time for your third Python challenge in this advent calendar. Your task is below:

Santa wants to give people a calendar so that they can be more time efficient. It is your job to make this.
Your program should do the following:

  • Have a menu to select between adding, viewing and deleting events.

  • Allow the user to delete and add events.
